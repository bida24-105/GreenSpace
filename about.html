About GreenScape

Our story, mission, and team

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by master landscaper David Mosenye, GreenScape began as a small family business with a passion for creating beautiful outdoor spaces.

What started as a modest operation with just two employees has grown into one of Botswana's most respected landscaping companies.

Our Mission

We believe that well-designed outdoor spaces enhance quality of life. Our mission is to bring beauty, functionality, and sustainability to every landscape we create, while providing exceptional service to our clients.
